What you get

Built for critical readers

🎨

Color-coded annotations

Rhetorical techniques are highlighted directly in your selected text — appeals, structural devices, and loaded language, each in a distinct color.

✏️

Neutral rewrite

Every analysis includes a plain, objective version of the text, plus an explanation of what the original author was doing and why.

💬

Word-level tooltips

Click any highlighted word to see a breakdown of its rhetorical role and alternative words that carry different connotations.

🔒

Your data stays local

Your API key is stored only on your device. No accounts, no tracking, no analytics. Text is sent only to Anthropic's API for analysis.

🦊

Chrome & Firefox

Available as a side panel on Chrome and a popup on Firefox. Same analysis engine, same results, on every major browser.

🎓

High school reading level

Explanations are written to be accessible, not academic. Designed for students, journalists, and anyone who reads critically.

How it works

1

Add your Anthropic API key

Open the extension settings and paste your key from console.anthropic.com. It's stored locally — never sent anywhere except Anthropic's own API.

2

Highlight 3–5 sentences

Select any text on any webpage — news articles, opinion pieces, social media posts, political speeches. Keep it to a few sentences for best results.

3

Read the analysis

The panel opens with your text color-coded by technique. Click highlighted words for detail. Scroll down for the neutral rewrite and explanation.

Privacy first

Neutralize collects zero personal data. No accounts. No analytics. No tracking.

The only external request made is sending your selected text to the Anthropic API — directly from your browser, not through any intermediate server.
